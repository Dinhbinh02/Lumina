Anki Management

Export vocabularies directly to Anki decks.

Getting Started

  • Step 1: Download and install Anki from AnkiWeb.
  • Step 2: Open Anki, navigate to Tools → Add-ons → Get Add-ons..., then install AnkiConnect by pasting the code: 2055492159.
  • Step 3: Restart Anki to activate the extension.

⚠️ Always make sure the Anki app is active on your device while using this function.
